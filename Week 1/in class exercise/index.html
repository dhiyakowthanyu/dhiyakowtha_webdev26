Heading 2- you can have as many h2- h6's as you want on the page

Heading 3- think hiearchy. What text is more important?

Heading 4- Thinnk of it as the bigger text in a research paper outline

Heading 5- you are usually good with h1-h3
Heading 6- smallest of headings
