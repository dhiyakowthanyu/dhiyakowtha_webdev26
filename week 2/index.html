Grilled Cheese Sandwich


A classic grilled cheese sandwich that is quick, comforting, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Materials

  • Medium Pan
  • Spatula

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread butter-side down in the pan.
  4. Add cheese on top.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread butter-side up.
  6. Cook until golden, then flip.
  7. Cook until cheese is melted.

Enjoy!!
